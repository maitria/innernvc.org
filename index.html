InnerNVC

History

Marshall Rosenberg, a student of Carl Rogers, wanted to find a way to decrease violence in the world. In 1960, he began putting together ideas, and creating the model that came to be called Nonviolent Communication. In 1999, his book by the same name was published.

What it is

InnerNVC is the name for an NVC practice that starts with the inner experience of the practitioner. We focus on awereness of what's happening inside our body, attention to the environment, our emotions, our ideas about the world and the people we're connected with.
